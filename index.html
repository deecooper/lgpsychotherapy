About

Lorna holds a BSc (Hons) in Counselling & Psychotherapy. My journey to becoming a Psychotherapist began when I returned to college as a mature student. Having previously worked in a customer service based role I developed a greater interest in human psychology. Growing up I was conscious of my own anxieties and low self-esteem issues. Through study and personal development I have realised the importance of growth and self-awareness and the ability to recognise when a little extra support is needed.

Services

Lorna uses an integrative approach in her therapy sessions focusing mainly on a Person-Centered approach which allows her clients the space to be open and free to talk about any concerns arising in a non-judgemental environment. She also uses Cognitive Behavioural Therapy as needed to give her clients that extra awareness of how our thinking patterns, body reactions and physical sensations are all linked together. Lorna has previous experience working with anxiety, low self-esteem & trauma. -Lorna offers a free initial 15-minute consultation to have a chat with potential clients and see if we are a good match. -Follow up appointments are €50-€60. This fee is negotiable taking into consideration the individual’s circumstances.
